About the Developer:

Haynes Brotherton is an IT staffing professional who comes from an education background. He is a graduate of North Carolina State University with a BA in History. Currently, he is working at the UNC Coding Bootcamp to become a Full-Stack Web Developer utilizing mainly the MERN stack while helping other software engineers find new opportunities through his recruiting day job.

Haynes is an avid golfer, and enjoys spending most of his free time on the course or relaxing with close friends.
